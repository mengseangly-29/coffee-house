Our Story

Our Legacy & Mission

Founded in the heart of Phnom Penh in 2026, The Coffee House became a sanctuary for coffee lovers, remote workers, and friends. We strongly believe that a great cup of coffee has the power to bring communities closer.

We prioritize organic farming and premium single-origin Arabica & Robusta coffee. Our ultimate mission is to serve excellence in every cup while supporting local and international sustainable agriculture.
